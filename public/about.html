Our Mission

Music Engine is dedicated to empowering artists, producers, and music industry professionals with cutting-edge copyright management tools. We believe that music is the weapon of intellectual property, and every creator deserves transparent, secure, and efficient rights management.

Technology Stack

Music Engine leverages state-of-the-art technology to ensure your intellectual property is protected and managed efficiently:

Blockchain Infrastructure: Built on secure, decentralized networks ensuring immutable copyright records and transparent transactions.

Smart Contracts: Automated execution of agreements, royalty distributions, and rights management without intermediaries.

Industry Standards: Full compliance with CWR 2.0, DDEX, Dublin Core, and other international music industry protocols.

Global Integration: Seamless integration with major performing rights organizations, publishers, and distributors worldwide.

Why Choose Music Engine?

Transparency: Every transaction, agreement, and rights transfer is recorded on the blockchain, providing complete transparency and audit trails.

Security: Military-grade encryption and blockchain security ensure your intellectual property is protected from unauthorized access or tampering.

Efficiency: Automated processes reduce administrative overhead and ensure faster royalty distributions and rights management.

Global Reach: Our platform supports international copyright laws and treaties, making it easy to protect your rights worldwide.

Cost-Effective: Reduced intermediary fees and automated processes mean more revenue stays with the creators.

Our Team

Music Engine is powered by a diverse team of music industry veterans, blockchain developers, legal experts, and technology innovators. Our combined expertise spans decades in music rights management, intellectual property law, and cutting-edge technology development.

We are committed to supporting the creative community and ensuring that artists and rights holders have the tools they need to succeed in the digital age.
