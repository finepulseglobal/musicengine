Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, register music, or contact us for support.

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Music files and metadata
  • Payment information
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process copyright registrations
  • Manage royalty distributions
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Improve our platform and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes blockchain technology for immutable copyright records.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
